Zurich AI & Machine Learning Meetup

Thursday, 15 May 2026

18:00 - 21:00

ETH Zurich, Main Building, Rämistrasse 101, 8092 Zürich

About this event

Join us for an exciting evening of AI and Machine Learning discussions! This meetup brings together data scientists, ML engineers, and AI enthusiasts from across Zurich.

We'll explore the latest developments in deep learning, neural networks, and natural language processing. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, there's something for everyone.

Agenda:

  • 18:00 - Networking and refreshments
  • 18:30 - Keynote: "The Future of AI in Switzerland" by Dr. Sarah Mueller, AI Research Lead at Google
  • 19:15 - Panel Discussion with experts from Microsoft, ETH Zurich, and local startups
  • 20:00 - Lightning talks and Q&A
  • 20:45 - Networking

Speakers:

Dr. Sarah Mueller, AI Research Lead at Google Zurich

Prof. Thomas Weber, Machine Learning Professor at ETH Zurich

Anna Schmidt, Data Science Manager at Microsoft

Topics covered:

AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Neural Networks, NLP, Computer Vision, Data Science, Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch
